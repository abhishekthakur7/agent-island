# Establish the authoritative parity inventory

## Question

What complete, evidence-linked inventory of user-visible behavior, settings, lifecycle rules, integrations, edge cases, and visual states constitutes the Parity Baseline after applying this map's explicit scope exclusions?

## Comments

### Resolution — 2026-07-18

The authoritative Parity Baseline inventory is [recorded in the supporting
asset](../assets/parity-baseline-inventory.md). It freezes evidence to Vibe
Island v1.0.42 and its public materials observed on 2026-07-18, applies every
Wayfinder exclusion, and limits first-class coverage to Claude Code, Codex CLI,
Cursor, iTerm2, Cursor's IDE/integrated terminal, Warp, and Orca.

The inventory has evidence IDs that trace to the local time-stamped research
record, separates required coverage from out-of-scope source features, and
marks unobserved action/accessibility states as explicit downstream questions
rather than invented parity requirements. It covers session aggregation and
lifecycle, island/list presentation, auto-reveal priority, Attention Requests,
Jump Back, notification/sound/filter/usage behavior, onboarding/Settings,
integration maintenance, and cross-cutting recovery and accessibility states.
